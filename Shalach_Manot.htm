Purim Shalach Manot Project

 

Congregation Anshai Torah invites you to participate in a wonderful annual tradition – the exchanging of gift baskets in celebration of Purim.  On the joyous occasion of Purim, it is customary to send food and gifts to family and friends.  This mitzvah of “Shalach Manot” is one of the many ways in which we, as a congregation, can emphasize the importance of community.  Proceeds from the Shalach Manot project will sustain the many ongoing activities of the synagogue including religious and educational programs.  These funds are vital to the financial wellbeing of our shul and benefit all of us.

 

How it works

 

Every Anshai Torah member will receive a Purim gift basket containing an assortment of goodies.  These are hand-delivered to our local members and associate members.  You sponsor baskets by choosing names from the attached membership list.  The recipient’s basket will include a card with your name on it.  You can also send to non-members by simply providing their name and address.

 

This year Purim begins on the evening of March 9th.  We’ll begin delivering baskets on Sunday, March 1st.  You’ll receive yours in time for Purim.

 

Sponsor baskets for all members, including “CATs in College”

 

For just $500 you can become one of our Shalach Manot Benefactors and sponsor for the whole congregation.  This is an easy and terrific way to support the shul and all the programs we offer.  Your name will appear on every basket delivered to Anshai Torah members (including associates) and staff.  Your name will also appear on baskets sent to approximately 100 member college students throughout the country.

 

Baskets for non-members

 

Do you have family or friends outside Anshai Torah?  Perhaps your parents or one of your children is living in another state.  No problem!  You have the option of shipping a “basket” to any U.S. address.  We’ll package it up and ensure it arrives in time for Purim.  The price is only $25.

 

Deadline for orders

 

Friday, February 21st is the deadline for all orders.  We cannot accept orders received after this date.

 

Delivery drivers needed!

 

Shalach Manot is made possible each year by a wonderful team of volunteers.  The area in which we need the most volunteers is basket deliveries.  If you can help, please check the box on the order form.
